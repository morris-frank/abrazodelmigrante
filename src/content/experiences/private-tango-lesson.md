## A lesson that meets you where you are

Private instruction works best when it removes pressure. The goal is not to overwhelm you with figures. It is to help you understand connection, balance, rhythm, and what makes tango feel distinct.

## Flexible focus

This lesson can stay very simple, or it can move into more musical detail for experienced dancers. That flexibility is why the page is set up as its own editable experience rather than a generic one-line service.

## Typical directions

- First encounter with tango for travelers who have never danced it before
- A grounding session before attending a milonga
- A confidence-building class for people who dance socially and want local feedback
