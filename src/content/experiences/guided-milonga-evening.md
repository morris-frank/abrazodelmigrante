## What this evening is for

This format is for people who want to understand what a milonga is from the inside. Instead of dropping into a venue cold, you arrive with enough context to read the room, follow what matters, and relax into the experience.

## What it can feel like

Some guests want a careful first introduction. Others already love tango music and want help finding the right place for their taste. The evening can be framed around observation, dancing, conversation, or simply absorbing the social rhythm of the city.

## A draft structure

- We choose the right neighborhood and venue for the evening.
- You get a clear explanation of customs, codes, and pacing before things become busy.
- Once inside, the guidance stays available but unobtrusive.
- If useful, you also leave with ideas for where to return on your own later in the trip.
