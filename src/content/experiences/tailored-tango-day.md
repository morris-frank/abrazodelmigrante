## For guests who want more than one moment

Sometimes a single lesson or single evening is not enough. A tailored day gives you space to connect the dots between tango, architecture, food, neighborhoods, and the social pace of Buenos Aires.

## A few possible directions

- Afternoon lesson followed by dinner and a milonga
- A neighborhood walk with historical tango references and time to pause
- A day designed for dancers who want help navigating local options
